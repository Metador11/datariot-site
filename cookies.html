Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience on Datariot.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for the platform to function (authentication, security) Session
Preferences Remember your settings (theme, language, feed preferences) 1 year
Analytics Help us understand how the platform is used to improve features 90 days
Performance Monitor platform performance and loading speeds 30 days

3. Local Storage

We also use browser localStorage to store your theme preference (dark/light mode). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database services
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent certain features from working properly.

6. Contact

Questions about our cookie usage? Contact us at privacy@datariot.com.
