Datariot Documentation

Learn how to use the Datariot educational platform — from Smart Feed to Debate Arena, Creator DNA to AI Sensei. Everything you need to get started.

Overview

Datariot is a next-generation educational platform that combines short-form video content with AI-powered learning and structured intellectual debate. Unlike traditional social media, Datariot optimizes for understanding, not watch time.

Smart Feed

AI-curated 60-second videos that adapt to your learning pace and interests.

Debate Arena

Structured debates replace comment sections. Build arguments, earn Logic Score.

Creator DNA

Your evolving profile that tracks your intellectual journey and content impact.

AI Sensei

Personal AI tutor that deepens your understanding of any video content.

Smart Feed

The Smart Feed is the core content discovery experience. It uses the Daily Synergy algorithm to curate a personalized stream of 60-second educational videos tailored to your Creator DNA profile.

Feed Modes

Datariot offers three distinct feed visualization modes:

  • Pulse Feed — Vertical swipeable videos with a DNA-inspired background animation. The primary mobile experience.
  • Mosaic Feed — Grid-based layout showing multiple video thumbnails. Great for browsing and discovering new content.
  • Classic Feed — Horizontal scrolling cards with preview information. A focused, information-dense view.

Daily Synergy

The "Daily Synergy" section appears at the top of your feed and highlights content that specifically matches your learning trajectory. It analyzes:

  • Your viewing history and engagement patterns
  • Topics you've debated and your argument quality
  • Your Creator DNA profile and knowledge gaps
  • Trending content within your interest categories
ℹ️ Note

The feed requires a minimum of 5 videos per section. If fewer are available for a category, the algorithm intelligently pads the section with related content.

Debate Arena

The Debate Arena is Datariot's answer to toxic comment sections. Instead of unstructured arguments, users engage in structured intellectual discourse with clear rules and scoring.

How It Works

  1. Propose a Thesis — Share a bold claim and stake your intellectual reputation. Example: "AI will replace 50% of jobs by 2035."
  2. Build Arguments — Others respond with structured arguments: evidence, counter-evidence, and logical reasoning. No ad-hominem attacks allowed.
  3. Community Voting — The community votes on argument quality, not popularity. Votes are weighted by the voter's Logic Score.
  4. Earn Logic Score — Your Logic Score rises as you demonstrate critical thinking. High-quality arguments earn more points.

Logic Score

Your Logic Score is a reputation metric that reflects your ability to construct well-reasoned arguments. It's calculated based on:

Factor Weight Description
Argument Quality 40% Community upvotes on your arguments
Evidence Usage 25% How well you cite sources and data
Debate Participation 20% Consistency and frequency of engagement
Win Rate 15% Percentage of debates where your side prevailed
⚠️ Warning

Ad-hominem attacks, spam, or bad-faith arguments will result in Logic Score penalties and potential account restrictions.

Creator DNA

Creator DNA is your unique profile on Datariot. It evolves as you learn, debate, and create content. Think of it as your intellectual fingerprint — a living representation of your knowledge, interests, and growth.

Profile Stats

Your Creator DNA profile displays real-time statistics:

  • Member Since — When you joined the platform
  • Total Impact — Combined views, shares, and engagement across all your content
  • Top Interest — Your most-engaged topic category
  • Global Rank — Your position in the global leaderboard
  • Activity Level — How active you've been in the last 30 days
  • Approval Rate — Percentage of your content that receives positive engagement

How DNA Evolves

Your Creator DNA is not static. It adapts based on:

  • Videos you watch and how deeply you engage with them
  • Debates you participate in and arguments you make
  • Content you create and how the community receives it
  • Categories you explore and knowledge gaps you fill

AI Sensei

AI Sensei is your personal AI tutor within Datariot. It's available on every video page — just tap the AI button to dive deeper into any topic you're watching.

Capabilities

  • Context-Aware Q&A — Ask questions about the video you're watching. AI Sensei understands the content and provides detailed explanations.
  • Deep Dives — Request deeper explanations of concepts mentioned in videos. AI Sensei connects ideas across your learning history.
  • Progress Tracking — AI Sensei remembers your learning journey and adapts its explanations to your current knowledge level.
  • Debate Preparation — Use AI Sensei to research arguments before entering a debate. It helps you find evidence and construct logical frameworks.
💡 Pro Tip

You can access AI Sensei from the full-screen video view by tapping the pulsing Datariot logo at the top of the screen.

Platform Support

Datariot runs on iOS, Android, and Web. The app uses a responsive layout system that automatically adapts between mobile (full-screen video feed) and desktop (three-column layout with sidebar and right panel).

Community

Datariot is built around the idea that learning together multiplies the power of every insight. The community is the backbone of the platform.

Community Guidelines

  • Respect — Treat every member as a fellow learner. Disagree with ideas, not people.
  • Quality — Prioritize depth over quantity. One well-researched video or argument is worth more than ten low-effort posts.
  • Honesty — Cite your sources. Acknowledge when you're wrong. Intellectual humility is a strength.
  • Growth — Focus on learning and helping others learn. This isn't a competition — it's a collaboration.
